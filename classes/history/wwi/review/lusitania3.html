Lusitania (continued)

Acts of heroism amongst both the survivors of the sinking and the Irish rescuers who had heard word of the Lusitania's distress signals brought the survivor count to 764

---

Reasons for sinking the Lusitania

The German Embassy had warned passengers: Travelers intended to embark for an Atlantic voyage are reminded that a state of war exists between Germany and her allies and Great Britain and her allies.... Vessels flying the flag of Great Britain or any of her allies are liable to destruction.

---

Recent research also shows that the ship was carrying arms to Britain.
