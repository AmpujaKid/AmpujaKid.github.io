Lusitania

British passenger liner

Sunk by German sub

1,200 died, including 128 Americans

---

Lusitania was nearing the end of her crossing, as she was scheduled to dock at the Prince's Landing Stage in Liverpool later that afternoon.

---

She was running parallel to the south coast of Ireland, when the liner crossed in front of U-20 at 2:10 p.m.

---

It was sheer chance that the liner became such a convenient target, since U-20 could hardly have caught the fast vessel otherwise.
