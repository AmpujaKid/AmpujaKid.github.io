Types of mixtures:

Heterogeneous

Hetero: Different

Unevenly mixed molecules (ex. sand)

Suspension: solids not fully dissolved or evenly mixed in a liquid (ex. orange juice with pulp)

Colloid: solids not fully dissolved but evenly mixed (ex. milk)

Homogeneous

Homo: Same

Made of multiple types of molecules evenly distributed

Examples: Gases dissolved in liquids, mixtures of 2 miscible (mixable) liquids

Solutions: solids not fully dissolved in a liquid (ex. salt water)
