How do you calculate the volume of matter?


First, you have to measure something

Second, you have to do a little math


Volume of a Solid

To find the volume of a rectangular object, measure the length, width and height. Multiply the length times the width and multiply the result by the height. The result is the volume.


Volume by Displacement

To find the volume of an irregular object, use the Displacement Procedure. This method is simple and accurate and is the most commonly used way of determining the volume of an irregular object.
